## Common Pitfalls
*   **Incorrect Reward Function:** The reward function must accurately reflect the firm's objectives (e.g., profit maximization).
*   **Non-Stationary Environment:**  If the underlying market conditions change drastically during training, the Q-table may not converge. Consider adaptive learning rates or experience replay.
*   **Exploration-Exploitation Balance:** Tune `epsilon` to find the right balance between exploring new prices and exploiting known profitable prices.
*   **State Representation:** The state must contain information relevant to the pricing decision (e.g., demand, competitor prices). A poor state representation will lead to suboptimal pricing.
*   **Action Mapping:** Ensure the action (price index) is correctly mapped to an actual price value.
